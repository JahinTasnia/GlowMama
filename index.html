Health & Wellness

Nutrition Guide

  • Folic acid: 400-800 mcg daily
  • Iron: 27 mg daily
  • Calcium: 1000 mg daily
  • Protein: 70-100g daily

Safe Exercise Recommendations

  • Walking: 30 minutes daily
  • Swimming: Low-impact cardio
  • Prenatal yoga: Flexibility and relaxation
  • Pelvic floor exercises

Mental Health Support

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation
  • Join support groups
  • Talk to a counselor
  • Maintain social connections

Common Pregnancy Symptoms

  • Morning sickness: Usually improves by second trimester
  • Fatigue: Rest when possible
  • Back pain: Use proper posture
  • Heartburn: Eat smaller, frequent meals
